Vimmi Indra

Artist based in New Delhi

Vimmi’s artwork has received recognition from the Hon’ble President of India, when he appreciated her work in an exhibition held in multipurpose hall of Rashtrapati Bhavan, on conclusion of an artists-in-residence camp held inpremises Presidential Estate. Her work now is a part of the Hon’ble President’s Rashtrapati Bhavan art collection. Vimmi has held several solo and group art shows in India and abroad. She regularly contributes to charity and auction shows looking for ways to give back to the society through 7 her artwork. She has also curated multiple art-exhibitions and camps while promoting emerging artists.
